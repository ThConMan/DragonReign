DragonReign

Turn the Dragon Egg into a trophy worth fighting over. It can't be hidden, it can't be hoarded forever, and holding it earns real prestige.

Paper 26.1+ Java 21+ Free & open source
Download Documentation GitHub

The idea

One egg. Everybody wants it. Nobody can cheat to keep it.

No hiding it

The Dragon Egg can only sit in a player's inventory or be placed in the world — never in a chest, ender chest, bundle, hopper, or any container. No stashing it away.

No sitting on it forever

If the keeper goes inactive — or just leaves the egg untouched too long — it respawns at the End portal so everyone gets another shot at it.

Find it nearby

Get within range of a placed egg and an action-bar compass points you to it — warmer as you close in. Vault it in your base, sure, but stumble close and the hunt is on.

Earn rewards for holding it

Actively hold the egg and you climb a configurable reward ladder — items, money, XP, even flight vouchers. A built-in Dragon Egg Rewards book (/dr rewards) lays out exactly what each hour pays. Lose the egg and the ladder resets for the next keeper.

Can't be AFK-farmed

Rewards and prestige only build while you're really playing. AFK pools, auto-clickers, and walking away are all caught — no free progress for standing still.

Become a Dragonlord

Hold the egg long enough across the season and you permanently earn the Dragonlord title — a gradient name tag in chat, tab, and above your head — plus a living aura of shimmering spirit-orbs that wander, crackle with livewire surges, and roar to life when you fight.

Never lost, never duped

There's always exactly one egg. A loose egg is fireproof and floats out of lava; throw it in the void and it's rebuilt at the End. Every dupe trick was hunted down and closed.

Full history + staff inbox

Every egg event is logged and browsable in-game. Suspicious transfers and cleanups alert your staff — and queue up for whoever's online next.

How it plays out

A simple loop that keeps the egg in motion.

1

Claim it

Beat the dragon, grab the egg, or take it off whoever has it. Whoever last placed or picked it up is its keeper.

2

Hold it

Defend it and keep playing. The longer you actively hold it, the bigger your rewards — and the closer you get to Dragonlord.

3

Don't lose it

Wander off or go inactive and a countdown starts — the egg returns to the End for the whole server to fight over again.

Plays nice with your stack

Soft, optional hooks — DragonReign runs fine on a bare server too.

PlaceholderAPI
Publishes the Dragonlord title via %dragonreign_title%.
TAB
Shows the title in the tab list and above-head nameplate.
CMI / EssentialsX
Uses their AFK status; built-in fallback when neither is present.
LuckPerms
Optional meta/prefix output for permission-based setups.
Vault
Pay money rewards through any economy.
Chat plugins
Optional native chat title for handlers that don't read PlaceholderAPI.

Drop it in and go

No setup required — sensible defaults out of the box, every rule toggleable in-game.
